### `> whoami`

I'm the founder of **[Due Gooder](https://duegooder.com)** — a student productivity platform that helps students stay on top of their academic workload with AI-powered tools, calendar sync, and LMS integration. Been shipping code since 2014.

- 🔭 Currently building **Due Gooder V2** — a complete rebuild with Next.js 15, React 19 & AI
- 🧠 Obsessed with developer experience, product design, and making tools people actually want to use
- 🌱 Exploring AI integrations, edge computing, and background job architectures
- 📍 Based in **Louisville, KY**
